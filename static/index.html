Features

GST-Correct PDFs

CGST/SGST/IGST breakdowns, HSN/SAC codes, and Indian Rupee amount-in-words — every invoice compliant out of the box.

Email Invoices

Send invoices as PDF attachments directly from the app. One tap, straight to your client's inbox.

Expense Tracking

Log business expenses, categorize them, and keep a complete picture of your finances in one place.

Client Ledger

Track outstanding balances, payment history, and credit notes per client — no spreadsheet needed.

Payment Recording

Record partial or full payments against invoices. Allocate payments across multiple invoices.

Credit Notes

Issue returns, adjustments, and discounts with proper GST treatment. Balances update automatically.
